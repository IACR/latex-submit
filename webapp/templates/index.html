This site is currently used for submissions of final versions of papers for IACR Communications in Cryptology. If you are looking to submit a paper to the journal, see the Call for Papers.

Papers submitted to the IACR Communications in Cryptology are required to use the iacrcc document class.

The goal of this site is to reduce the cost of open access publishing by reducing the amount of human effort required by both authors and editors in the production of a paper. Once a paper is accepted, authors will be directed to a URL on this site in order to submit their final versions in LaTeX format. Their sources will be compiled, and after some light copy editing, the paper will be ready for publication within seconds rather than weeks or months.
