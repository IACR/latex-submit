Metadata search

This is designed to help you locate identifiers for the macros \affiliation and \addfunding in iacrcc.cls. ROR IDs are intended for use in \affiliation. FUNDREG identifiers are intended for \addfunding, but you may also use ROR IDs if you received funding as a visitor. Search is performed as you type. Help is available.

Help on metadata search

The \addfunding and \affiliation macros in iacrcc.cls can use IDs that globally identify organizations.

The \affiliation macro expects an ID from the Research Organization Registry (ROR). Not every organization will have such an ID, but there are now 102,000 organizations that have been registered.

The \addfunding macro may take one of two kinds of IDs:

  • An ID from the Crossref Funder Registry (FUNDREG) is preferred. This includes almost 45,000 funding agencies.
  • If you receive support from an organization to visit them and they have a ROR ID, then you may include that.
