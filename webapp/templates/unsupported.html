LaTeX packages that are unsupported

We explicitly do not support some LaTeX packages on this system because they invoke \write18, \ShellEscape, or potentially unsafe lua code. This is enforced by running lualatex in a sandbox. Some of the packages listed below may retain part of their functionality, but you should not rely upon them.
